For families

Beta

        

          
How to talk
After Reachy's greeting, say Hey Hermes, Okay Nabu, or Hey Reachy. Kids can interrupt with any wake phrase.

          
Adult supervision
Generative AI can still make mistakes. Keep a grown-up nearby, especially for younger children or sensitive topics.

          
Fast child voice
Replies use ElevenLabs Flash v2.5 and begin playing from streamed PCM as soon as audio arrives.

          
Cloud processing
Child audio/text—and the optional nickname in the greeting—go to the configured STT provider, OpenAI moderation/chat, and ElevenLabs speech. No Hermes memory does not mean provider non-retention; review those providers' terms.

          
Emergency boundary
Reachy is not an emergency service, therapist, or babysitter. In danger, contact a trusted adult and local emergency services.

          
Stop always wins
End now cancels voice and motion, clears queued speech, then uses Reachy's verified safe fold before torque release.
